Reinildo Isnard Mandava (born 21 January 1994) is a Mozambican international footballer who plays for Portuguese club Fafe, on loan from Benfica, as a defender.

Career

Mandava began his career with Ferroviário da Beira. He signed for Portuguese club Benfica B in December 2015.

He made his international debut for Mozambique in 2014.
