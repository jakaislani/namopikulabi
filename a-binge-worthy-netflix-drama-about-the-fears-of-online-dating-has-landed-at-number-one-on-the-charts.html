A binge-worthy Netflix drama about the fears of online dating has landed at number one on the charts

Netflix‘s suspenseful approach to the dangers of online dating in the series Fake Profile has captivated fans worldwide since its debut, so much so that it has topped the streaming platform’s global chart.

Recommended Videos

For context, Fake Profile follows a woman named Camila Román who is on a quest to find a significant other by creating a profile on Spice, a dating app. But much to her surprise, Fernando Castell, the man that Román is head over heels for, isn’t who he says he is. While trying to expose the truth, Román finds herself in an unconventional situation filled with sex, lies, and deception. The hit show stars Carolina Miranda, Rodolfo Salas, Juanse Díez, Manuela González, Víctor Mallarino, Mauricio Hénao, Lincoln Palomeque, Felipe Londoño, Juliana Galvis, and many more.

According to Netflix’s Top 10 Global chart, Fake Profile, which premiered on May 31, has generated over 76 million hours viewed on the streaming platform under the category of a non-English television show. In addition to dominating the non-English television show category, Fake Profile was the second most-watched show on the platform overall for the period of May 29 to June 4.


As Fake Profile began to trend online, mainly on Twitter, many expressed why they enjoyed watching the series. One individual mentioned how messy the series is.


At the same time, another claimed they were intrigued by the “many twists” that occurred in  the show.


A third person reiterated a similar statement and revealed Fake Profile‘s plot twists left them hooked.

Another social media user raved over how “scandalous” Fake Profile is.

If you were hoping to discover what all the fuss is about for yourself, Fake Profile is now streaming on Netflix.
