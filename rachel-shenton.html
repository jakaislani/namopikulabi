Rachel Shenton: Career

Small recurrent roles in different television programs, including Holby City and Waterloo Road, marked the start of Shenton’s acting career.

She has acted in two British films and starred in a number of television commercials for the Ministry of Defence, DFS, and Sega Superstars Tennis.

It was revealed in April 2010 that Shenton had been chosen to play ambitious beauty model Mitzeee Minniver in Hollyoaks. Shenton stated that her character was the exact opposite of her.

Shenton co-wrote and co-produced the movie The Silent Child with her husband Chris Overton, which debuted in theaters in August 2017.
