Gary Beadle is an English actor. He was born on 22nd March 1988. He is known for his wonderful performances in films and television series. He is known for appearing in the MTV reality series named ‘Geordie Shore’ started in 2011 and ended in 2017.

The actor hails from Prudhoe, Northumberland where he grew up and studied. After completing his studies, he then started looking for work in the industry as he wanted to pursue acting as a career.
