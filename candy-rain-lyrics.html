About

“Candy Rain” is a song by the R&B group Soul for Real, released in 1994.

It was their debut single and became a hit, reaching number two on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

The song has a smooth and soulful sound, with lyrics about falling in love and wanting to spend time with someone special.

It remains a popular song from the mid-1990s and has been covered by other artists over the years.
