Becky Hammon Net Worth

Becky Hammon is a WNBA guard who was born on March 11, 1977 in Rapid City, SD. She was a 6-time All-Star for the New York Liberty and San Antonio Silver Stars between 2003 and 2011. In 2014, she made history by becoming the first female coach in NBA history when she was hired to be an assistant coach for the San Antonio Spurs.
