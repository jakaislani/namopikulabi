Seth Rollins Is Ready For The Fiend Bray Wyatt Ahead Of WWE Hell in a Cell

Seth Rollins vs ‘The Fiend’ Bray Wyatt is the match that most fans are getting hyped for ahead of WWE Hell in a Cell, and rightly so.

The build-up has been interesting to say the least, with Seth quite clearly being afraid of The Fiend and everything that comes alongside his appearances. Now, though, he needs to step up – because otherwise, Wyatt will walk all over him.

Despite the seemingly impossible odds that face him, both with the bookmakers and with the fans, Seth is more than ready to face whatever comes his way.

It certainly has been a nightmare for Seth Rollins in regards to the treatment that he’s been faced with at the hands of Wyatt. Unfortunately, despite how much hype there was for his Universal Championship reign when he first won it, nobody seems to be interested in the idea of his time as champion continuing.

That’s probably because The Fiend has taken off in a way that few could’ve imagined. He could quite easily hold the belt all the way through until WrestleMania if he does wind up winning it, and that’s what we’d put our money on.

There are so many great challengers for Bray if he does become champion but before all of that, we need to remember some of the best moments from Rollins’ reign – because it may be a while before he gets it back.

Either way, there is a great deal of potential in the actual match itself on Sunday.
