Park Hae-soo aka Hae-soo Park is a South Korean actor and model. As of 2023, Park Hae-soo’s net worth is $4 million. He is in the industry since the year 2007 and works with BH Entertainment. He is most known in South Korea for his portrayal as Cho Sang-woo in the Netflix drama Squid Game.

Mister Lobby marked Park’s musical theatrical debut in 2007. Additionally, he performs in other musical productions, like Angel Called Desire and Annapurna. In 2017, he made a strong impression with his main performance in the drama series Prison Playbook, earning him the Seoul Awards’ Best New Actor award.
