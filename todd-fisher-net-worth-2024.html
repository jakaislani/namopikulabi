Early Life and Career Beginnings

Todd Fisher was born into a family of entertainers, with his mother being the legendary actress Debbie Reynolds and his sister the iconic Carrie Fisher. He began his career in the entertainment industry at a young age, appearing in his mother’s Las Vegas stage act and later on television shows.
