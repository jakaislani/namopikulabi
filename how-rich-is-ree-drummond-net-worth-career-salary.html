How Rich Is Ree Drummond? Net Worth, Career, Salary

Big Bad Budget Battle, an American cuisine cable channel, is scheduled to premiere another reality cooking competition. Home chefs competing in this culinary competition will have to manage their finances as well as their time. The program will challenge participants against one another to come up with inventive, delectable cuisine on a budget. The participants must next go grocery shopping and prepare two meals: breakfast and an enhanced one-pot supper. Ree Drummond, the Pioneer Woman, will host the program and assess the candidates with a rotating team of judges.

How much money does Ree Drummond have? Meet the Big Bad Budget Battle host.

Ree Drummond, an American blogger, author, food writer, photographer, television personality, and celebrity chef, is 53 years old. Her net worth is reported to be $ 50 million, as of April 2023, after 11 years on The Pioneer Woman and various side projects. Ree Drummond, the presenter of Big Bad Budget Battle, began blogging in 2006. She talked about homeschooling and gave detailed, educational recipes that were simple to follow and included visual hints.

Her site began as Confessions of a Pioneer Woman and was then renamed Pioneer Woman, earning her several honors such as Weblog of the Year in 2009 and 2010, as well as being named one of Time Magazine’s “25 Best Blogs” in the world in 2009. The Big Bad Budget Battle host is also the author of six New York Times best-selling cookbooks, including Frontier Follies and The Pioneer Woman Black Heels to Tractor Wheels. She has also authored a number of children’s books.

She made her television debut in an episode of Throwdown! with Bobby Flay, in which she challenged and defeated the celebrity chef in a Thanksgiving cook-off. Pioneer Woman began on the Food Network in 2012. Ree Drummond and her husband opened The Merc, a retail restaurant in downtown Pawhuska, in 2016. In 2018, they also opened The Boarding House, a bed and breakfast. In downtown Pawhuska, Oklahoma, they also opened Charlie’s Sweet Shop, an ice cream parlor, and P-Town Pizza.

Big Bad Budget Battle will be hosted and judged by Ree Drummond.

Ree Drummond will host the newest Food Network reality program, which will debut on August 9, 2022, at 10 p.m. ET. The program will run on the Food Network, and episodes will be available to watch on Discovery+ the same day.
